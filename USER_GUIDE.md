## Getting Started

Before using Budglet, ensure the application has been successfully installed by following the **Installation Guide**. New users should register for an account before logging in. Returning users can sign in using their existing email address and password. After successfully logging in, Budglet opens to the Home Dashboard, where users can begin managing their personal finances. 

## Logging In

To access Budglet:

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **Login**.

If the credentials are valid, the application opens the Home Dashboard. If an incorrect email address or password is entered, Budglet displays an error message and prompts the user to try again. 

![Login Screen](images/login.png)


## Home Dashboard

The Home Dashboard provides an overview of your financial activity and serves as the application's main navigation page. 

From the dashboard, users can:

- View account balances
- Review income and expenses
- Monitor recent financial activity
- Navigate to the Budgeting, Calendar, and Profile pages

As financial information is entered, the dashboard updates to provide a summary of the user's finances. 

![Home Dashboard](images/homepage.png)



## Budgeting

The budgeting page allows users to create and manage personal budgets.

From this page, users can:

- Create budgeting goals
- Monitor spending progress
- Compare current spending against budget limits
- View financial information throught charts and visual summaries

Regularly updating budget information helps maintain an accurate picture of overall financial health.

## Frequently Asked Questions 

### I can't log in?

Verify that your email address and password are entered correctly. If you continue experiencing issues, confirm that your account has been created successfully. 

### Why don't reports or budget summaries appear?

Budget summaries and financial reports become more useful after income, expenses, budgets, and financial goals have been entered into the application.



### Can I edit my budget?

Yes. Budgets can be updated as your financial situation changes, allowing you to maintain accurate spending goals. 



### Where can I manage my account?

Account information and settings can be accessed through the **Profile** page.
